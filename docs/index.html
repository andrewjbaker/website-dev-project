Liverpool FC


About:

Liverpool Football Club is an English football team based in Liverpool, in the north west of England. The men's professional team currently plays in the English Premier League, the top tier in English football, and plays its home matches at Anfield stadium. The team is managed by Jurgen Klopp, a German football manager who has been with the team since 2015.

Liverpool FC has a rich history, and regularly features in European knockout competitions held by UEFA. Sadly, the club has also been involved in tragedies, namely the Hillsborough and Heysel stadium disasters, where football fans lost their lives.
